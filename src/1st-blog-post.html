Closing One Door to Open Another

December 3, 2025

I have a big announcement to make. After six years, I have decided to permanently close my co-working space in Banha at the end of this month (December 2025).

This wasn't an easy decision, but it is a necessary one.

Since 2019, I have been in pursuit of a career in Data. I got my big break in 2023 when I joined the first cohort of the ALX Cairo Data Science program. It was an incredible experience, yet a year after finishing the program, I haven't landed a single interview despite actively applying.

I realized I needed to be honest with myself. My co-working space, while a great chapter in my life, had become a "comfort zone." It was chaining me to Banha and holding me back from fully committing to the opportunities available in the wider tech market.

Sometimes, you have to let go of "good enough" to chase what you really want.

I am entering 2026 with a clear mind and a singular focus. I am now fully dedicated to my Data Science journey, unanchored and ready to relocate or work remotely. I am also igniting a passion for 3D modeling, which I will be practicing regularly.

To my connections: I am open to work, eager to interview, and ready to bring six years of entrepreneurial grit to a data team.
